Our Security Systems
02

Every safeguard. Shown, not told.

Here is every system we run in your property — the exact tool, the exact process, and what it means for you as a property owner. This is what separates us from every other operator who approaches you.

01

Identity Verification — Every Guest, Before Check-In

Before any guest receives access, they complete a full ID verification process. They submit a photo of their government-issued ID and a live selfie holding that ID. Our system cross-references the submission against a sex offender database and verifies their email and phone number are real and deliverable. This gives us a forensic-level identity record of every person who has ever been in your property.

For you: If anything ever happens and law enforcement needs to identify who was in the property — we have it. Name, face, ID number, contact information. All documented before they ever touched your door.
02

Noise Monitoring — Real-Time Decibel Alerts

We install a Minut noise monitoring device in every property. Minut measures decibel levels in real time and sends us an immediate alert the moment sound levels exceed a threshold consistent with a party or large gathering. It does not record conversations or audio — only decibel levels — making it fully privacy-compliant. Guests know it is there from the moment they book.

For you: You will never receive a noise complaint call from a neighbor. We handle it before it becomes your problem. The device is a visible deterrent — guests who know they are being monitored do not throw parties.
03

Smart Lock Access Control — Every Entry Logged

We install a smart lock on every property. Each guest receives a unique, time-limited access code that activates at check-in and expires automatically at checkout. No physical key is ever handed over. Every entry and exit is logged with a timestamp. Access can be revoked remotely in seconds. A former guest can never re-enter your property after their stay ends.

For you: You always know who has access to your property. We can pull the full access log at any time. Your property is never accessible to someone who shouldn’t be there.
04

Guest Screening — Not Everyone Gets Approved

Our listings require verified government ID, a minimum review threshold, and a pre-stay questionnaire. We do not accept same-day bookings from unknown guests. We target professional travelers — remote workers, business travelers, relocated professionals, and traveling healthcare workers — whose employers have already vetted them before we do.

For you: The random party-seeking guest never makes it past our booking requirements. Our typical guest has a job, a professional license, or a company paying their housing. They treat properties with respect because that is simply how they operate.
05

No-Party Policy — Written, Acknowledged, Enforced

Every listing prominently displays a no-party policy. Every guest acknowledges it before booking is confirmed. Our house rules are displayed inside the property on arrival. If a guest violates the policy, their stay is terminated immediately — we remotely deactivate their access code and escalate to the platform for removal.

For you: Your neighbors will never know a short-term rental is operating next door. Our guests are quiet professionals who need to be at work the next morning.
06

STR Insurance — A Real Policy, Not a Platform Promise

We carry a dedicated short-term rental liability insurance policy through Proper Insurance — the leading STR-specific insurer in the United States. This policy covers property damage, liability, and loss of income. It is entirely separate from Airbnb’s AirCover program. Our policy is in the name of Taino Collective LLC and is in force before the first guest arrives. You are named as an additional interested party.

For you: You have a real insurance policy — not a platform promise — covering your property. If anything happens, there is a clear claims process through a licensed insurer.
07

Professional Cleaning — After Every Single Guest

Between every guest, a professional cleaning crew brings the unit to hotel-grade standards. Linens laundered, surfaces sanitized, consumables restocked, maintenance issues identified and reported. A cleaning checklist is completed and any damage photographed immediately — before the next guest arrives.

For you: Issues are caught early and handled immediately. Your property never deteriorates because it is never left unattended for months at a time.
Common Questions
03

Every question you have.

Who is actually staying in my property?

Every guest is ID-verified with a government ID and live selfie before they receive access. We cross-reference against a sex offender database. We have a full digital record of every person who has ever stayed — name, face, and contact information. You are never dealing with anonymous strangers.

What if someone throws a party?

Our Minut noise monitor alerts us the moment decibel levels rise above normal. We respond immediately. If unresolved, we remotely deactivate the guest’s smart lock and escalate to the platform. Our no-party policy is written, acknowledged by every guest before booking, and displayed inside the unit.

What if something gets damaged?

We carry a Proper Insurance STR liability policy — a real insurance policy in our LLC name, not Airbnb’s AirCover. Normal wear is handled at our cost. Significant damage goes through a licensed insurer with a clear claims process. Our cleaning team documents every turnover so damage is identified and attributed immediately.

How do I know you’ll pay rent on time?

Your rent is paid on the 1st of every month by ACH transfer. Whether we have a guest that month or not — your rent does not change. You are not absorbing our occupancy risk. That is entirely ours. This is in writing in the lease before we sign anything.

Is this legal?

Yes. Rental arbitrage with short-term subletting is a legally established business model operating in all 50 states. We operate with your explicit written permission in the lease. The subletting clause is fully disclosed, reviewed by both parties, and compliant with Rhode Island law. We hold all required STR permits before the first guest arrives.

What if I need the property back?

We include a 60-day written early termination clause on both sides. If your circumstances change, written notice starts the clock. We honor all existing bookings within that window and return the property professionally cleaned and documented.
