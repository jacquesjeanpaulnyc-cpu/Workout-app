Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how WaxOS, operated by Lux Skyn Haus LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website at waxos.netlify.app or use our services.

Information We Collect

When you join our founding group waitlist or use our services, we may collect the following information:

  • First and last name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business name
  • Business size (number of active clients)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your waitlist registration and founding member application
  • Communicate with you about onboarding, product updates, and founding group information
  • Send SMS messages if you have opted in (see SMS section below)
  • Improve our products and services
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

SMS Messaging and Phone Numbers

If you opt in to receive SMS messages from WaxOS / Lux Skyn Haus LLC, your phone number will be used solely for the purposes you consented to: onboarding communications, founding group updates, product announcements, and account-related notifications.

We will never sell, share, rent, or transfer your phone number or SMS opt-in data to third parties or affiliates for their marketing purposes. SMS opt-in data and consent are not shared with any third party.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP or contact us at info@waxos.app.

By providing your phone number and checking the SMS consent box on our waitlist form, you confirm that you are the subscriber or authorized user of the phone number provided and consent to receive recurring SMS messages from WaxOS.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is stored securely and access is limited to authorized personnel only.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as Netlify for hosting and form processing, and Twilio for SMS messaging) to operate our website and deliver communications. These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may only use it to provide services on our behalf.

We do not sell your personal information to any third party for any purpose.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Opt out of email communications by clicking the unsubscribe link

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy
